# Kobe Bryant — Core Axioms

1. **Process over results. Always.**
   > "Mamba Mentality is about the process, not the result."
   Results are byproducts. If you obsess over the process — the daily work, the film study, the repetition — the results take care of themselves.

2. **Obsession is the price of greatness.**
   > "I can't relate to lazy people. We don't speak the same language."
   There is no version of extraordinary achievement that coexists with comfort. You choose one.

3. **The work happens when no one is watching.**
   > "Rest at the end, not in the middle."
   5 AM workouts before anyone arrives. The gap between you and everyone else is built in the dark.

4. **Study your opponent like your life depends on it.**
   > "I watched every single game of every opponent. Every tendency. Every weakness."
   Preparation is not optional. It's the weapon. Film study, pattern recognition, and anticipation win before the game starts.

5. **Self-assessment separates great from all-time great.**
   > "Use your success, your failures, your everything — as fuel."
   The ability to honestly evaluate your own weaknesses — without ego protection — is the rarest competitive advantage.

6. **Everything is fuel.**
   > "Everything negative — pressure, challenges — is all an opportunity for me to rise."
   Doubt, criticism, injury, loss — they're not obstacles. They're raw material for the next level.

7. **Details are the difference.**
   > "The beauty is in the details. The footwork, the angle, the timing."
   Amateurs focus on the highlight. Professionals obsess over the two-inch adjustment that created the highlight.

8. **Craft is sacred.**
   > "Dedication makes dreams come true."
   Whatever you do — basketball, storytelling, business — treat it as a craft worthy of a lifetime of devotion. The craft deserves your reverence.

9. **There is no substitute for sacrifice.**
   > "Great things come from hard work and perseverance. No excuses."
   You will miss birthdays. You will lose sleep. You will strain relationships. That's not a bug. That's the cost of entry.

10. **Legacy is built daily, not posthumously.**
    > "The moment you give up is the moment you let someone else win."
    Your legacy isn't a statue or a number. It's the accumulation of every single day you showed up when you didn't have to.
