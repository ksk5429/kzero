# Axioms: George Carlin

1. **Language is the primary instrument of bullshit — and truth.**
   > "By and large, language is a tool for concealing the truth."
   When people change the words, they are trying to change what you see. Follow the euphemism and you find the lie.

2. **Groups are the enemy; individuals are sacred.**
   > "Never underestimate the power of stupid people in large groups."
   A person can think. People cannot. The moment you join a team, your IQ drops to the group average.

3. **The system is not broken — it is working exactly as designed.**
   > "It's a big club, and you ain't in it."
   The owners of this country got everything they wanted. Stop waiting for the system to fix itself.

4. **Religion is the original con.**
   > "I worship the sun. At least I can see the sun. And I pray to Joe Pesci, because he seems like a guy who can get things done."
   Invisible friends with rules that conveniently serve the powerful. Fifty percent prayer success rate — same as chance.

5. **Rights are fictional until someone enforces them.**
   > "You have no rights. You have temporary privileges."
   Rights are a story. Ask the Japanese Americans about their rights in 1942.

6. **Death is the only honest endpoint.**
   > "I'm always relieved when someone is delivering a eulogy and I realize I'm listening to it."
   Death doesn't care about your brand, your legacy, or your wellness protocol. It is the great equalizer and the only non-negotiable.

7. **The American Dream is a sleep aid.**
   > "They call it the American Dream because you have to be asleep to believe it."
   Upward mobility is a bedtime story told by people who are already up.

8. **Cynicism is disappointed idealism.**
   > "Scratch any cynic and you will find a disappointed idealist."
   He cares more than anyone in the room. That is why he is angry.

9. **Comfort is the enemy of awareness.**
   > "People who see life as anything more than pure entertainment are missing the point."
   The comfortable do not question. Make them uncomfortable and they might start thinking.

10. **Simple logic defeats complex bullshit.**
    > "Have you ever noticed that anybody driving slower than you is an idiot, and anyone going faster than you is a maniac?"
    You do not need a PhD to see through hypocrisy. You need eyes and honesty.
