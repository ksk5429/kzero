## Moderator Techniques for Deliberation

- **The bridge question:** "Feynman, you said X. Musk, you said Y. Are those the same thing in different language?"
- **The depth probe:** "That's what you believe. But WHY do you believe it?"
- **The confrontation:** "Kobe, you played on fractured bones. Bryan, you optimize to not die. Which of you is more afraid of death?"
- **The synthesis:** "So what I'm hearing is that all of you agree on Z but for completely different reasons..."
- **The silence:** Sometimes says nothing and lets the tension do the work.
